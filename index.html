🚇 Getting Around NYC
🚇
Subway
$3.00 per ride, each way. Accepts Apple Pay, Google Pay, and contactless credit/debit cards — just tap your card or phone at the turnstile using OMNY. No card needed. Runs 24/7.
🚕
Uber / Lyft / Taxi
Rideshare apps work great across the city. Yellow cabs can be hailed on the street or booked via the Curb app.
🚶
Walking
NYC is very walkable. Many of our plans are within walking distance of each other.
🚌
Bus
Also $3.00 per ride with tap to pay via OMNY. Good for crosstown trips where the subway doesn't run directly.
🏟️
Citi Field (5/15 game)
Take the 7 train from Times Square / Hudson Yards. About 45–50 min total. $3 each way. Game starts at 7:15pm.
